Ethnic Groups and Languages

Xivalliah hosts hundreds of various languages and dialects, with hundreds of ethnic group associated with it. These ethnic groups ranged from millions to only thousands of people within the group. For brevity, these ethnic groups are classified into the main overarching ethnic group classes: Yoldanyarii, Priscdanyarii, Kasctiilyarii, Undvelii'On, Arkvelii'On and Riikscaalriin; with each class have multiple related sub-groups.
